## Patile akantu. 
## Insala yaliponene mu calo, abantu ifya kulya tabalekwata iyoo.  
## Awe abaume ilyo baupile abanakashi kwisa landa  
## “we mukashi wandi apo insala ya cilamo, kankulobolemo ilinso.”
## Abakashi bebele, “kuti mwandobolamo shani ilinso?”  
## Ati, “Awe ili line ilinso lyobe, tukaya libyala mu mpanga, pakuti likaletwala.”  
## Awe efyo umukashi asumine, bamulobola ne linso.  
## Awe efyo baile byala ilinso mu mpanga, no kumena lyamena.  Likulafye, likulafye.
## Awe mukwai talyakokwele nokutwala, lya amba nokutwala.
## Abakashi bebele, “awe balume bandi katukamone ilinso limbi kuti lya twala?”
## Ati iyoo, we mwine wa linso teti uye pitako pantu kuti uleumfwa icifukushi ku mulandu wa linso lyobe. 
## Abalume bebele, “ine naya nkalenguleko!” Elyo abalume baimine baya mukulengula ilinso. 
## Awe basanga ilinso cine na lipya no kupya.  
## Bayamba ukwimba ulwimbo.
